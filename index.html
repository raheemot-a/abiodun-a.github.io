About

Operations as a discipline.
Execution as a craft.

I began my career in 2010 as an Administrative Secretary at S.P.A. Ajibade & Co, one of Nigeria's foremost law firms. What started as mastering schedules and correspondence grew into a deep expertise in how organisations actually function: the invisible infrastructure that separates firms that scale from firms that stall.

Over fifteen years, I have held progressively senior roles spanning operations, HR, facility management, executive support, and people leadership. I have managed multi-functional offices, led recruitment and performance frameworks, administered payroll and pension schedules, and served as the operational backbone for partners and executives navigating complex, high-stakes environments.

"Every organisation has a rhythm. My work is to find it, protect it, and amplify it."

Today, I operate as an independent executive services professional and agency, partnering with business leaders in Nigeria, the UK, the US, and Canada. I help clients build the operational infrastructure, people practices, and administrative systems that allow them to lead at their highest level.

Beyond the office, I have planned end-to-end events from venue sourcing and catering through to aso-ebi coordination, usher management, and gift curation. I served as Public Relations Officer at Queen's College Lagos, one of Nigeria's most prestigious secondary institutions. These experiences form the breadth of a professional who understands that excellence in execution is always relational.
